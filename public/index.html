Biography

Robert C. Cline, Sr. is a Certified Professional Landman. He has experience managing land brokerage firms and field work. He specializes in Leasing, Title, Due Diligence, and GIS mapping. His interests include QGIS for GIS mapping, MS Database management, Data Analytics using Power BI, Power Query, DAX language, and application development using Excel VBA and Access VBA.
